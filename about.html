About MyPharmAlly

Our Story

MyPharmAlly was founded by Jibran and co-founded by Kamran Nazeer, with a vision to make healthcare access simpler and more dependable for everyone. It is a product of Noor Fatima Healthcare Group, an initiative that started as a small local pharmacy.

The foundation of this journey was laid by Dr. Qaisar War, whose dedication and commitment to helping people inspired everything that followed. What began as one pharmacy became a larger mission to serve the community through trust and care.

Our Aim

At MyPharmAlly, our aim is simple — to offer a smooth, honest, and accessible experience for anyone who needs medicines or health support. Whether it’s uploading a prescription or talking to someone for guidance, we want it to feel effortless and human.

We are not just building a platform; we are building relationships — where people feel safe, supported, and respected.

Our Values

  • ✔️ Transparency in pricing and service
  • ✔️ Respect for every customer and prescription
  • ✔️ Fast, safe, and reliable medicine delivery
  • ✔️ Compassionate customer support
  • ✔️ Locally rooted, digitally connected
