About MyPharmAlly

At MyPharmAlly, our mission is to provide a trusted, fast, and easy way to get certified medicines delivered to your doorstep 24/7.

Our Values

  • Customer-first approach
  • Certified quality medicines
  • Transparency and trust
  • Fast and reliable delivery

Our Team

Our team is composed of experienced pharmacists and healthcare professionals dedicated to your wellness.

Our Story

MyPharmAlly is a heartfelt initiative by Noor Fatima Healthcare Group, aimed at easing the lives of people — especially the elderly — who often walk miles just to get their daily medicines. With the grace of the Almighty, we are committed to solving everyday healthcare and medicine challenges with dignity and compassion.

The idea was brought to life in 2025 by War Jibran, Founder of MyPharmAlly and CEO of Noor Fatima Healthcare Group, along with Kamran Nazeer, Co-founder and a dedicated force behind the platform’s development and execution.

However, the true foundation of this journey lies in the vision of Dr. Qaisar War, the original founder of Noor Fatima Healthcare Group — whose lifelong dedication to public health continues to inspire everything we do today.

This beautiful platform emerges from the serene valley of Kashmir, built with love, honesty, and the intent to serve. At MyPharmAlly, we don’t just deliver medicines — we deliver care, relief, and hope to those who need it most.
